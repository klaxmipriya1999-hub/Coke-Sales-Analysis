# Coke-Sales-Analysis
This sales analysis project focuses on identifying the key factors affecting beverage sales across different retailers, regions, brands, and months using the “Key American Coca-Cola Retailers” dataset for the year 2021. The analysis reveals that Coca-Cola and Dasani Water were the highest-selling beverage brands, while Fanta recorded the lowest sales.

Among retailers, Sodapop generated the highest revenue and showed a nationwide presence compared to other retailers operating in limited regions. Region-wise, the West achieved the highest sales performance, whereas the Midwest recorded the lowest sales during 2021. Monthly sales analysis indicated peak sales during months 6 and 12, while month 3 experienced the lowest sales. 

Overall, the project demonstrates how factors such as brand popularity, seasonal trends, retailer presence, and regional demand influence total sales performance.
